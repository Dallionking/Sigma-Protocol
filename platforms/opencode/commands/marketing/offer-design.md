# Marketing Offer Design Command

Design irresistible offers using Hormozi + NESB frameworks.

## Usage

```
/marketing/offer-design
/marketing/offer-design --type=core
/marketing/offer-design --type=grand-slam
```

## Parameters

- `--type`: core | grand-slam | tripwire | downsell (default: grand-slam)
- `--price`: price point for context

---

## The Value Equation (Hormozi)

```
            Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood
Value = ────────────────────────────────────────────
            Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice
```

**Maximize numerator, minimize denominator.**

| Factor | Score 1-10 | How to Improve |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| **Dream Outcome** | ___ | Make transformation more vivid, specific |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | ___ | Add proof, guarantees, testimonials |
| **Time Delay** | ___ | Emphasize speed of results |
| **Effort & Sacrifice** | ___ | Reduce steps, simplify process |

## NESB Offer Enhancement (Kyle Milligan)

Every offer must trigger all four emotions:

### NEW: The Unique Mechanism

> "Give them something they haven't seen before"

- [ ] Named your proprietary method?
- [ ] Explained why it's different from alternatives?
- [ ] Positioned as discovery/innovation?

**Template:** "The [Adjective] [Mechanism Name] that [outcome]"

### EASY: Perceived Simplicity

> "If it looks hard, they won't start"

- [ ] Clear step count? (3 is ideal)
- [ ] Time estimate stated?
- [ ] Complexity removed from customer view?

**Template:** "[Number] simple steps to [outcome] in [time]"

### SAFE: Risk Reversal

> "Remove every reason to say no"

- [ ] Guarantee type defined?
- [ ] Social proof prominent?
- [ ] "Risk-free" language used?

**Risk Reversal Options:**

| Type | Power | Template |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Unconditional** | ★★★★★ | "100% money back, no questions asked" |
| **Conditional** | ★★★★☆ | "If you [action] and don't [result], full refund" |
| **Performance** | ★★★★★ | "We work free until you hit [outcome]" |
| **Anti-Guarantee** | ★★☆☆☆ | "No refunds - serious buyers only" |

### BIG: Outcome Magnitude

> "The transformation must be worth the pain of change"

- [ ] Outcome specific and measurable?
- [ ] Transformation clearly articulated?
- [ ] Before/After contrast vivid?

**Template:** "Go from [painful before] to [dream after] in [time]"

## Unity/Tribe Positioning (Cialdini)

Answer these questions to build tribe identity:

1. **Who is "us"?**
   - What do your customers call themselves?
   - What tribe do they belong to?

2. **Who/What is the enemy?**
   - What frustrates them about status quo?
   - Who profits from their struggle?

3. **What movement are you building?**
   - What do you stand FOR?
   - What belief unites your customers?

**Unity Statement Template:**
> "[Brand] is for [specific identity] who believe [core belief] and are tired of [common enemy]. We're building [movement/outcome] together."

## Five Drivers in Offer Messaging

| Driver | Offer Element | Copy Approach |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **Encourage Dreams** | Outcome description | "Yes, you CAN achieve [dream]" |
| **Justify Failures** | Why this is different | "Past failures weren't your fault" |
| **Allay Fears** | Guarantee section | "Worried about [fear]? Here's protection" |
| **Confirm Suspicions** | Problem statement | "You were right about [suspicion]" |
| **Throw Rocks** | Differentiation | "[Enemies] don't want you to know" |

## Offer Validation Checklist

**NESB Score (must be 4/4):**
- [ ] NEW: Unique mechanism named and explained
- [ ] EASY: Simplified to 3-step process
- [ ] SAFE: Guarantee + proof in place
- [ ] BIG: Specific, measurable transformation

**Value Equation (target 8+/10 each):**
- [ ] Dream Outcome: ___/10
- [ ] Perceived Likelihood: ___/10
- [ ] Time Delay (inverted): ___/10
- [ ] Effort & Sacrifice (inverted): ___/10

**Five Drivers Addressed:**
- [ ] Dreams encouraged
- [ ] Failures justified
- [ ] Fears allayed
- [ ] Suspicions confirmed
- [ ] Enemy identified
