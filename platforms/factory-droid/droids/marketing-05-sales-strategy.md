## 🔄 Phase E: NEPQ Sales Scripts (Miner 2025)

### Discovery Call Framework

```markdown
## NEPQ Discovery Call

### Opening (2 min)
"Hey [Name], thanks for booking. Before we dive in - 
what made you book this call today?"
[Let them talk - this reveals intent]

### Connecting Questions (3 min)
- "Tell me about your business/situation"
- "What are you focused on right now?"

### Situation Questions (5 min)
- "Walk me through a typical day/week"
- "What have you tried so far?"
- "What's working? What isn't?"

### Problem Awareness Questions (5 min)
- "What's the main thing holding you back?"
- "How long has this been going on?"
- "What's that costing you?" (time, money, stress)

### Solution Awareness Questions (5 min)
- "What would solving this look like?"
- "If this was handled tomorrow, what changes?"
- "What would that be worth to you?"

### Consequence Questions (3 min)
- "What happens if nothing changes in 6-12 months?"
- "How does that make you feel?"
[Let silence work]

### Transition to Solution (5 min)
- "Based on what you've shared, here's how we help..."
- [Match their problems to your solutions]
- [Stack the value]

### Close (3 min)
- "Does this sound like what you need?"
- "Want to get started?"
```

### NESB Validation for Sales Copy (Kyle Milligan Framework)

Before finalizing any sales copy, ensure it triggers all four emotional responses:

**Score Each Piece of Copy (1-10):**

| Trigger | Question | Your Copy Score |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| **NEW** | Does this feel like a novel discovery? | ___/10 |
| **EASY** | Does this feel achievable without heroic effort? | ___/10 |
| **SAFE** | Have you removed all perceived risk? | ___/10 |
| **BIG** | Is the promised transformation significant? | ___/10 |

**NESB Sales Copy Examples:**

| Element | Without NESB | With NESB |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| **Headline** | "Our coaching program" | "The 3-Phase Protocol that took me from $0 to $1M" |
| **Subhead** | "Learn sales techniques" | "Close deals in 15 minutes using questions—no scripts, no pressure" |
| **Proof** | "Many clients succeed" | "1,247 students with 68% avg close rate—guaranteed" |
| **CTA** | "Sign up now" | "Get instant access—start closing this week" |

### Five Drivers Objection Framework (Blair Warren)

Use the Five Drivers to preemptively address objections in your copy:

| Driver | Hidden Objection | Copy Response |
|--------|------------------|---------------|
| **Encourage Dreams** | "Is this really possible for me?" | "You're closer than you think. [Name] was exactly where you are..." |
| **Justify Failures** | "I've failed before—maybe I'm the problem" | "If you've tried before and failed, it wasn't your fault. You were using..." |
| **Allay Fears** | "What if this doesn't work?" | "You're protected by our [guarantee]. The only way you lose is by not trying." |
| **Confirm Suspicions** | "I bet this is just like all the others" | "You suspected something was off with [old way]. You were right..." |
| **Throw Rocks at Enemies** | "Who/what has been holding me back?" | "The gurus who told you [common advice]? They forgot to mention..." |

---

### Top 10 Objection Handlers (Enhanced with Five Drivers)

```markdown
### "I need to think about it"
> "Totally understand. What specifically do you want to think through?
> Is it the investment, timing, or whether this will work for you?"
> **[Five Drivers: Allay Fears - address the real hesitation]**

### "It's too expensive"
> "Compared to what? What's the cost of NOT solving this over 6-12 months?"
> **[Five Drivers: Confirm Suspicions - they suspect they're losing more by waiting]**

### "I don't have time"
> "That's exactly why you need this. You don't have time BECAUSE of 
> [the problem]. Once handled, you GET time back."

### "I need to talk to my partner"
> "Makes sense. What do you think they'll want to know? 
> I can help you answer those questions now."

### "I've tried this before"
> "What went wrong? [Listen] Here's why this is different..."
> **[Five Drivers: Justify Failures - their past failure wasn't their fault, they had the wrong approach]**

### "Not the right time"
> "When would be the right time? What would need to change?"

### "Can you send more info?"
> "Of course. What specifically would you like to know? 
> I can probably answer it right now."

### "What if it doesn't work?"
> "That's why we have [guarantee]. If [conditions], then [terms]."
```
