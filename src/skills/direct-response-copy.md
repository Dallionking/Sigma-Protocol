# Direct Response Copy Skill

Write copy that converts. Apply proven direct response frameworks to landing pages, emails, ads, and sales pages. Every word earns its place.

## When to Invoke

Invoke this skill when:
- Writing landing page copy
- Creating email sequences
- Writing ad copy (Facebook, Google, etc.)
- Building sales pages
- Crafting CTAs
- Any copy where conversion is the goal

---

## 🎯 Core Principles

### 1. One Reader, One Goal
- Write to ONE specific person
- Drive ONE specific action
- Remove everything that doesn't serve the goal

### 2. Benefits Over Features
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| "256-bit encryption" | "Your data is safer than Fort Knox" |
| "AI-powered" | "Get answers in seconds, not hours" |
| "Cloud-based" | "Access from anywhere, on any device" |

### 3. Clarity Over Cleverness
If they have to think, you've lost them.

### 4. Specificity Sells
| Vague ❌ | Specific ✅ |
|----------|-----------|
| "Save time" | "Save 4.5 hours every week" |
| "Many customers" | "12,847 teams trust us" |
| "Fast results" | "Results in 14 days or less" |

## 📋 Headline Formulas

### The Proven Formulas

**How To:**
```
How to [achieve desired outcome] without [pain point]
How to [achieve desired outcome] in [timeframe]
```
Examples:
- "How to Double Your Revenue Without Hiring a Single Salesperson"
- "How to Learn Spanish in 90 Days (Even If You Failed High School Spanish)"

**The Question:**
```
Do you [experience problem]?
What if you could [achieve dream outcome]?
```
Examples:
- "Do You Make These Mistakes in English?"
- "What If You Could Work 4 Hours a Day and Make More Money?"

**The Secret/Reason:**
```
The Secret to [desired outcome] That [authority] Don't Want You to Know
[Number] Reasons Why [surprising claim]
```
Examples:
- "The Secret to Perfect Skin That Dermatologists Don't Tell You"
- "7 Reasons Why Your Startup Will Fail (And How to Beat the Odds)"

**The Command:**
```
Stop [doing wrong thing]. Start [doing right thing].
[Do this] to [get result]
```
Examples:
- "Stop Wasting Money on Ads That Don't Convert"
- "Download Our Free Guide to 10X Your Email Open Rates"

**The New/Announcing:**
```
Introducing: [product] — The [category] That [unique benefit]
Finally: A [product] That Actually [delivers promise]
```
Examples:
- "Introducing: The CRM That Sells For You"
- "Finally: A Project Management Tool That Doesn't Suck"

### Headline Power Words

**Urgency:** Now, Today, Immediately, Instant, Fast, Quick
**Value:** Free, Save, Bonus, Exclusive, Limited, Secret
**Emotion:** Amazing, Discover, Unlock, Transform, Revolutionary
**Trust:** Proven, Guaranteed, Research-backed, Tested
**Curiosity:** Why, How, What, Secret, Hidden, Little-known

## 🏗 Landing Page Framework

### The AIDA Structure

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ATTENTION — Headline that stops scroll │
│  (Above the fold, <3 seconds to hook)   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  INTEREST — Expand on the promise       │
│  (Subheadline, key benefits)            │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  DESIRE — Make them want it             │
│  (Features → Benefits, Social proof)    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  ACTION — Tell them what to do          │
│  (Clear CTA, reduce friction)           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### The PAS Structure

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  PROBLEM — Identify the pain            │
│  "Tired of [frustrating situation]?"    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  AGITATE — Twist the knife              │
│  "It gets worse..."                     │
│  (Make them feel the pain deeply)       │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  SOLVE — Present your solution          │
│  "There's a better way..."              │
│  (Position as the obvious answer)       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Landing Page Template


## ✉️ Email Copy Framework

### Subject Line Formulas

**Curiosity Gap:**
- "The [thing] I wish I knew sooner..."
- "This changes everything about [topic]"
- "I was wrong about [topic]"

**Benefit-Driven:**
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "[Number] ways to [desired outcome]"
- "Your [problem]? Solved."

**Personal/Story:**
- "Quick story about [relatable situation]"
- "My biggest [mistake/lesson] with [topic]"
- "What happened when I [did thing]..."

**Urgency/Scarcity:**
- "[Time remaining]: [offer]"
- "Last chance: [offer expires]"
- "Only [number] spots left"

### Email Structure

```markdown
**Subject:** [Hook that creates curiosity]
**Preview:** [Expands on subject, creates more intrigue]

---

Hey [Name],

[Opening hook — relatable story, surprising fact, or question]

[Bridge to the problem/opportunity]

[Present the solution/offer]

[Benefits and proof]

[Call to action]

[P.S. — reinforce offer/add urgency]
```

### Welcome Sequence Template

**Email 1: Welcome**
- Thank them for joining
- Set expectations
- Deliver promised content
- Ask one engaging question

**Email 2: Story + Value**
- Share origin story
- Provide useful content
- Build relationship

**Email 3: Problem Agitation**
- Identify their pain
- Show you understand
- Tease the solution

**Email 4: Solution Introduction**
- Present your product/service
- Benefits over features
- Soft CTA

**Email 5: Social Proof**
- Testimonials
- Case studies
- Results

**Email 6: Offer**
- Full pitch
- Clear CTA
- Urgency/scarcity if applicable

## 📱 Ad Copy Framework

### Facebook/Instagram Ad Structure

```markdown
**Hook (Stop the scroll)**
[Question, bold claim, or pattern interrupt]

**Problem**
[1-2 sentences identifying pain point]

**Solution**
[Introduce product as the answer]

**Proof**
[Quick social proof element]

**CTA**
[Clear action with benefit]
```

```

(And probably making costly mistakes.)

StockFlow automatically syncs your inventory across all channels.
Real-time updates. Zero data entry.

Join 5,000+ e-commerce brands who've already made the switch.

→ Start your free trial today
```

## 🔥 CTA Best Practices

### Button Text That Converts

**Value-focused (best):**
- "Get My Free Guide"
- "Start Saving Time"
- "Claim Your Spot"
- "Show Me How"

**Action-focused (good):**
- "Start Free Trial"
- "Download Now"
- "Sign Up Free"

**Weak (avoid):**
- "Submit"
- "Click Here"
- "Learn More"

### CTA Formula

```
[Action Verb] + [Benefit/Deliverable]
```

Examples:
- "Get Instant Access"
- "Start My Free Trial"
- "Download the Checklist"
- "Reserve My Seat"
- "Unlock Premium Features"

## 💎 Persuasion Techniques

### Social Proof Types

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| **Numbers** | "12,000+ customers" |
| **Testimonials** | Customer quotes with results |
| **Logos** | Company/publication logos |
| **Ratings** | "4.9/5 on G2" |
| **Awards** | Industry recognition |
| **Case Studies** | Detailed success stories |

### Urgency & Scarcity

**Time-based:**
- "Offer ends Friday"
- "48 hours left"
- "Limited time only"

**Quantity-based:**
- "Only 7 spots remaining"
- "Limited to 100 users"
- "While supplies last"

**Exclusivity:**
- "Founding members only"
- "Invite-only access"
- "Not available in stores"

### Risk Reversal

- "30-day money-back guarantee"
- "Try it free for 14 days"
- "No credit card required"
- "Cancel anytime"
- "If you don't see results, you don't pay"
