## Executive Summary

**Trading Platform** is a minimalist, AI-driven Forex trade automation mobile app designed for traders who want passive income from the markets without manual trading complexity. The app features a distinctive black OLED interface with indigo neon accents—creating a unique "cyberpunk meets trading" aesthetic that no competitor offers.

Users connect their broker account via TradeLocker, select a risk level (Safe/Balanced/Aggressive), and activate the AI Trading Bot with one tap. The AI Trading Assistant executes automated trades via server-side Python scripts, providing a truly "set and forget" trading experience across Forex, indices, commodities, and crypto markets.

## Problem Statement

### The Problem

Millions of aspiring traders want to profit from Forex and financial markets but are paralyzed by:
- **Decision fatigue** from analyzing charts, indicators, and market conditions
- **Intimidation** from complex trading platforms like MT4/MT5 with steep learning curves
- **Lack of time** to monitor 24/5 markets and execute trades manually
- **Distrust** of trading bots that feel scammy or require technical coding skills

They want to participate in trading profits, but every existing solution requires them to become a professional trader first.

### Pain Points (Ranked by Severity)

1. **"Trading platforms are too complex"** (Severity: 10/10) — MT4/MT5 overwhelm new traders
2. **"I don't have time to watch the markets"** (Severity: 9/10) — 24/5 Forex markets require constant attention
3. **"I can't code my own trading bots"** (Severity: 9/10) — Algo trading is inaccessible to non-developers
4. **"Most trading bots look like scams"** (Severity: 8/10) — Poor UX and shady marketing erode trust
5. **"Prop firm challenges are too stressful"** (Severity: 7/10) — High failure rates discourage traders

### Market Validation

- **$7.5 trillion/day** traded in Forex markets—massive opportunity
- **TradeLocker** gaining rapid adoption as next-gen trading platform (faster than MT4/MT5)
- **Prop trading firms** on TradeLocker growing (30+ firms listed on TradeLocker Hub)
- **#ForexTrading** has 5B+ views on TikTok—massive demand signal
- **Gap identified:** No competitor combines modern design + ultra-simplicity + AI trading automation

## Target Platforms & Technical Considerations

### Primary Platform
- **iOS (iPhone)** — Primary target, full-width design optimized for OLED displays
- **Minimum iOS:** 15.0+
- **Target Devices:** iPhone 12 and newer (OLED optimization)

### Secondary Platform (Future)
- **Android** — Phase 2 expansion after iOS market validation
- **Web Dashboard** — Phase 3 for account management only

### Technical Architecture Considerations

**Scalability:**
- Support 10,000 concurrent users at launch
- Scale to 100,000+ within Year 1
- Real-time portfolio balance and trade status updates

**Integrations:**
- **Broker Connection:** TradeLocker API for broker/prop firm account linking
- **Trade Execution:** Server-side Python scripts via TradeLocker REST API
- **Subscription Billing:** Stripe/RevenueCat for subscriptions
- **Push Notifications:** Critical for engagement (trade events, profit updates)

**Performance:**
- App launch: <2 seconds
- Portfolio refresh: Real-time with WebSocket
- Animation smoothness: 60fps minimum

**Security:**
- SOC 2 compliance (target)
- End-to-end encryption for trading data
- Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
- JWT token storage via expo-secure-store (TradeLocker credentials never stored)

## Non-Functional Requirements

### Performance
- [ ] App launch time: <2 seconds
- [ ] Balance refresh: <500ms (WebSocket)
- [ ] Animation frame rate: 60fps minimum
- [ ] Offline mode: Display cached balance, queue actions

### Scalability
- [ ] Launch users: 10,000 concurrent
- [ ] Year 1 target: 100,000 users
- [ ] Data storage: ~1KB per user per day (events, status)

### Security
- [ ] Authentication: OAuth2 / JWT with biometrics
- [ ] Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
- [ ] Compliance target: SOC 2 Type II
- [ ] Broker data: TradeLocker JWT tokens stored in expo-secure-store, never in plain storage
- [ ] Session management: Token refresh per TradeLocker spec, logout on suspicious activity

### Accessibility
- [ ] WCAG 2.1 AA compliance (minimum)
- [ ] VoiceOver support for all interactive elements
- [ ] High contrast mode (indigo on black is already high contrast)
- [ ] Reduced motion option for animations

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| TradeLocker API changes/downtime | Medium | High | Monitor API status, implement graceful degradation |
| Animation performance on older iPhones | Medium | Medium | Test on iPhone 11, implement LOD |
| Real-time trade sync reliability | Low | High | WebSocket + polling fallback |
| Trading script execution failures | Medium | High | Robust error handling, position monitoring |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Regulatory scrutiny on automated trading | Medium | Critical | Clear disclaimers, users maintain control, legal review |
| Users expect guaranteed profits | High | High | Clear risk disclaimers, education in onboarding |
| Trading strategy underperforms | Medium | High | Multiple strategies, transparent performance metrics |
| Broker compatibility issues | Medium | Medium | Focus on TradeLocker-verified brokers |
| High churn after losing trades | Medium | High | Risk management focus, drawdown limits |

### User Experience Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Users distrust "black box" AI | Medium | High | Transparency about allocation (not strategy), trust signals |
| Aesthetic alienates mainstream | Low | Medium | Future "light mode" option |
| Users choose extreme risk | Medium | Medium | Safe caps, warnings, impact tooltips |
