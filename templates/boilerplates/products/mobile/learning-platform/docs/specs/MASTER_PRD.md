## Problem Statement

### The Problem

**62 million Americans** want to learn effectively, but **95% fail** with existing solutions because apps teach recognition (matching words), not production (speaking). Users complete years of Duolingo yet freeze when actually spoken to in the subject.

### Pain Points (Ranked by Severity)

| Rank | Pain Point | Impact | Evidence |
|------|------------|--------|----------|
| 1 | **Recognition vs. Production Gap** | Users "know" the subject but can't speak it | Duolingo 1.7★ on Trustpilot, user reviews cite this |
| 2 | **No Human Connection** | AI feels robotic, users quit after 1-2 weeks | 90%+ app abandonment rate in language learning |
| 3 | **Inauthentic the subject** | Textbook the subject doesn't match real conversations | Users can't understand colloquialisms, slang |
| 4 | **No Accountability** | Solo learning lacks motivation | Average streak: 3 days before abandonment |
| 5 | **Expensive Tutors** | Quality 1:1 instruction costs $35-50/hour | Most can't afford consistent practice |

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| AI costs spike with user growth | Medium | High | Usage caps per tier, caching common responses |
| Video call quality issues | Medium | High | LiveKit has proven reliability, fallback to external links |
| App Store review delays | Medium | Medium | Submit early, follow guidelines strictly |
| Speech recognition accuracy | Low | Medium | Use OpenAI Whisper (industry leading) |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| AI Tutor becomes capacity constrained | High | High | AI scales teaching, group classes, VIP tier caps |
| Competition from well-funded apps | Medium | Medium | Authenticity + human connection = moat |
| Content creation bottleneck | Medium | Medium | Start with existing 40-60%, batch production |
| Low conversion rate | Medium | Medium | Strong onboarding, clear value demo, 7-day trial |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Single point of failure (AI Tutor) | High | High | AI augmentation, recorded content, eventual team |
| Customer support overwhelm | Medium | Medium | In-app help, FAQ, community support |
| Technical issues during live calls | Low | High | Pre-call checks, backup contact method |
