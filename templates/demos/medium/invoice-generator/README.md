# Invoice Generator Demo

A freelancer invoicing tool with PDF generation and payment tracking.

## Overview

Build a professional invoicing application for freelancers and small businesses to create, send, and track invoices with automatic payment reminders.

## Target Audience

- Freelancers
- Consultants
- Small business owners
- Independent contractors

## Complexity

**Medium** - 1-2 days

## Key Features

1. **Client Management**
   - Add/edit clients
   - Client contact details
   - Payment terms per client
   - Client history

2. **Invoice Creation**
   - Professional invoice template
   - Line items with quantities
   - Tax calculation
   - Discounts
   - Notes and terms
   - Multiple currencies

3. **PDF Generation**
   - Download as PDF
   - Email invoice directly
   - Custom branding/logo
   - Invoice numbering

4. **Payment Tracking**
   - Mark as paid/partial/overdue
   - Payment due dates
   - Automatic reminders
   - Payment history

5. **Dashboard**
   - Outstanding invoices total
   - Revenue this month/year
   - Overdue alerts
   - Recent activity

## MVP Scope (for 1-2 day build)

Focus on:
- Client CRUD
- Invoice creation with line items
- PDF download
- Payment status tracking
- Simple dashboard

## Success Metrics

- Invoice created in < 5 minutes
- PDF generates in < 3 seconds
- Clear payment status overview

## Example Use Cases

1. Freelance developer invoicing clients monthly
2. Consultant billing for project milestones
3. Small agency tracking multiple client payments
