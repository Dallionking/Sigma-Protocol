# Newsletter Platform Demo

A Substack-style publishing platform with paid subscriptions.

## Overview

Build a newsletter publishing platform that allows writers to publish content, grow their audience, and monetize through paid subscriptions.

## Target Audience

- Writers and journalists
- Content creators
- Industry experts
- Thought leaders
- Independent publishers

## Complexity

**Full** - 1 week+

## Key Features

1. **Writer Tools**
   - Rich text editor
   - Draft management
   - Scheduling posts
   - SEO settings
   - Custom themes

2. **Publishing**
   - Free and paid posts
   - Email delivery
   - Web archive
   - RSS feeds
   - Social sharing

3. **Subscriptions**
   - Free subscriber list
   - Paid subscription tiers
   - Stripe integration
   - Subscriber management
   - Cancellation handling

4. **Analytics**
   - Open rates
   - Click tracking
   - Subscriber growth
   - Revenue dashboard
   - Popular content

5. **Reader Experience**
   - Clean reading interface
   - Comment system
   - Bookmark/save posts
   - Email preferences
   - Mobile optimized

6. **Discovery**
   - Publication landing page
   - Search functionality
   - Related posts
   - Author profiles

## MVP Scope (for 1 week build)

Focus on:
- Writer registration and publication setup
- Rich text editor with basic formatting
- Free and paid post publishing
- Email delivery to subscribers
- Basic subscription management
- Publication landing page

## Success Metrics

- Writer publishes in < 30 minutes
- Email delivered in < 5 minutes
- Subscription conversion trackable

## Example Use Cases

1. Tech industry newsletter
2. Personal finance commentary
3. Local news publication
4. Industry analysis reports
