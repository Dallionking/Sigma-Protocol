# Fitness Coaching Demo

A personal trainer platform with client management and workout plans.

## Overview

Build a comprehensive fitness coaching platform that connects personal trainers with clients, featuring workout plan creation, progress tracking, and client communication.

## Target Audience

- Personal trainers
- Fitness coaches
- Gym owners
- Health and wellness professionals
- Their clients

## Complexity

**Full** - 1 week+

## Key Features

1. **Trainer Dashboard**
   - Client roster management
   - Workout plan library
   - Session scheduling
   - Progress overview
   - Revenue tracking

2. **Client Management**
   - Client profiles with goals
   - Body measurements tracking
   - Progress photos
   - Health questionnaires
   - Communication history

3. **Workout Plans**
   - Exercise library
   - Custom workout builder
   - Templates for common programs
   - Video/image demonstrations
   - Assign to clients

4. **Progress Tracking**
   - Weight/measurement logging
   - Workout completion
   - Before/after photos
   - Strength progression
   - Charts and visualizations

5. **Communication**
   - In-app messaging
   - Workout feedback
   - Check-in reminders
   - Progress celebrations

6. **Client Portal**
   - View assigned workouts
   - Log workout completion
   - Track personal metrics
   - Message trainer
   - View progress history

## MVP Scope (for 1 week build)

Focus on:
- Trainer registration and profile
- Client invitation and management
- Basic workout plan builder
- Workout assignment
- Client workout view and logging
- Simple progress tracking

## Success Metrics

- Trainer creates workout in < 15 minutes
- Client logs workout in < 2 minutes
- Clear progress visualization

## Example Use Cases

1. Independent personal trainer managing remote clients
2. Gym trainer with in-person and online clients
3. Group fitness coach with standard programs
4. Sports coach tracking athlete performance
