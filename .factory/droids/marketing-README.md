## 🎯 The Difference: Scripts vs. Outlines

### Old Way (Outlines) ❌
```
• Talk about productivity
• Mention 3 tips
• Add a call to action
```

### New Way (Full Scripts) ✅
```
[0:00 - VISUAL: Close-up face, direct eye contact]

"What if I told you everything you know about productivity is a lie?

I spent 6 months testing every productivity system out there. 
$5,000 in courses. 50+ books. 

And here's what I discovered: The 'gurus' are wrong.

In the next 10 minutes, I'm going to show you the 3 things that 
ACTUALLY work—and none of them involve waking up at 5 AM.

Let's dive in."

[0:30 - VISUAL: Cut to screen recording, music fades in]
```
