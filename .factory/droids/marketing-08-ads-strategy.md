## 🏆 2025 Platform Landscape (Research-Validated)

### Meta/Facebook Ads (2025)

**Key Experts to Follow:** Jon Loomer, Mari Smith, Barry Hott, Travis Moh, Ezra Firestone

**Key 2025 Changes (From Research):**

1. **Signal-Based Optimization is King**
   - Targeting doesn't matter as much anymore
   - **Creative is the new targeting** - each video/image teaches Meta who your buyers are
   - First-party signals are critical

2. **Advantage+ Dominates**
   - 65% of advertisers now scale profitably using Advantage+ campaigns
   - Consolidated structures see **32% drop in CPA**
   - Fewer campaigns, more budget per campaign

3. **AI Does the Heavy Lifting**
   - Meta invested billions in AI-powered systems
   - New interface rewards **ease and efficiency over over-complication**
   - Old micro-targeting rules are gone

4. **Benchmarks (2025):**
   - Average CTR: 0.90%
   - Top performers (retail/beauty/fitness): 1.16-1.59% CTR
   - Broad targeting + first-party signals = best results

**Research Query:** `"Meta ads best practices 2025 Advantage+"`

### TikTok Ads (2025)

**Key Experts to Follow:** Maxwell Finn, Savannah Sanchez (DTC creative)

**Key 2025 Insights (From Research):**

1. **Beyond Entertainment**
   - TikTok is now a **cultural engine and commerce platform**
   - Users spend **90+ minutes daily** on the app
   - Ad revenue projected to hit **$39.7 billion by 2026**
   - TikTok US ad spend: **$11.8 billion** (up 21% from 2024)

2. **Native-Looking Creative Wins**
   - Don't plan formats first; plan **hand-offs (hook → proof → offer)**
   - Authenticity > Production value
   - Talking head + captions = winning combo
   - Trending sounds boost performance

3. **Intent Surfaces Expanding**
   - Search ads now available
   - Instant Lead Forms
   - TikTok Shop integration

4. **What Works (2025):**
   - First 2 seconds decide EVERYTHING
   - Sound-on by default
   - User-generated content (UGC) style
   - Localize in the opening line

## 🔄 Meta/Facebook Strategy (2025)

### Campaign Structure (Advantage+ Era)

```
CAMPAIGN (CBO - Campaign Budget Optimization)
│
├── Advantage+ Shopping/App (if applicable)
│   └── Let Meta's AI optimize everything
│
├── Ad Set: Broad Targeting (PRIMARY)
│   ├── Ad 1: Hook A + Body A
│   ├── Ad 2: Hook A + Body B
│   └── Ad 3: Hook B + Body A
│
└── Ad Set: Retargeting
    ├── Website visitors (180 days)
    ├── Video viewers (75%+)
    └── Engaged users
```

### 2025 Best Practices

1. **Simplify Structure**
   - Fewer campaigns = better results
   - Consolidate audiences
   - Let Advantage+ do targeting

2. **Creative is the New Targeting**
   - Each creative trains the algorithm
   - Test concepts, not micro-audiences
   - Volume of creative > volume of ad sets

3. **Signal Quality**
   - Conversions API (CAPI) essential
   - First-party data priority
   - Track meaningful events, not clicks

## 🔄 Creative Testing Framework (2025)

```markdown
## Concept → Hook → Body Testing

PHASE 1: Concept Testing ($100/concept)
├── Test 3-5 different ANGLES
├── Same audience, different messages
├── Winner = best CTR

PHASE 2: Hook Testing ($50/hook)
├── Take winning concept
├── Test 5+ different HOOKS (first 3 sec)
├── Winner = best hold rate

PHASE 3: Body Testing ($50/body)
├── Take winning hook
├── Test different BODY content
├── Winner = best conversion

PHASE 4: Scale
├── Combine winning elements
├── Scale budget gradually
├── Introduce new concepts monthly
```
